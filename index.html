    My Process

    Do Research

    Figuring out what the topic is really about.

    Gathering Inspo

    Figuring out what I want to create, going on Pinterest for aesthetic choices.

    Creating Drafts

    Creating a checklist of what is expected of me, creating small thumbnails of everything to be included.

    Fleshing out details

    Choose one out of all, with a set color scheme, brushes and consistency.

    Gather Feedback and Revise

    Send the detailed version out, figure out what needs to be fixed and keep on revising until the final product.

    Completion

    After fixing multiple drafts, complete the artwork, but always be open to feedback and continuous small changes.
